Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 31, 2026

👋 Introduction

Welcome to Daily Money Tracker. We are committed to protecting your personal information and your right to privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your data when you use our app.

By using Daily Money Tracker, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

📋 Information We Collect

We collect the following information when you create an account and use the app:

  • Account information: Your name, email address, and country
  • Financial data: Income and expense entries you manually enter
  • Savings goals: Goals and progress you set in the app
  • Recurring entries: Scheduled transactions you set up
  • App preferences: Language, currency, and theme settings

We do NOT collect: Bank account details, credit card numbers, government ID, or any sensitive financial credentials.

🎯 How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide and maintain the Daily Money Tracker service
  • Sync your data across all your devices
  • Personalize your experience (language, currency)
  • Send daily reminders if you enable notifications
  • Improve the app based on usage patterns
  • Respond to your support requests

🔒 Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored securely using Supabase, a trusted cloud database provider. All data is:

  • Encrypted in transit using HTTPS/TLS
  • Encrypted at rest in our database
  • Protected by Row Level Security — only YOU can access your data
  • Stored on secure servers in the United States

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

🤝 Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share your data only in these limited cases:

  • Supabase: Our database provider who stores your data securely
  • Legal requirements: If required by law or legal process
  • Safety: To protect the rights and safety of our users

🍪 Cookies & Analytics

Daily Money Tracker may use basic analytics to understand how users interact with the app. This helps us improve the experience. We do not use tracking cookies for advertising purposes.

If we add advertising in the future, we will update this policy and notify users.

👶 Children's Privacy

Daily Money Tracker is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will delete it.

🌍 International Users

Daily Money Tracker is available worldwide including Afghanistan, Turkey, Saudi Arabia, UAE, and other countries. By using the app, you consent to your data being transferred to and processed in the United States where our servers are located.

  • Delete: Request deletion of your account and data

To exercise these rights, contact us at the email below.
